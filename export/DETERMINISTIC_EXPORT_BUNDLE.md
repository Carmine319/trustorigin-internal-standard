# Deterministic Export Bundle Specification

## Purpose

Export bundles ensure migration survivability independent of vendor ecosystems.

---

## Bundle Components

Each bundle may include:

- Canonical artefact representation
- Integrity hash (SHA-256 or equivalent)
- Parent lineage reference
- Timestamp metadata
- Vendor dependency declaration
- Freeze state indicator (if active)

---

## Design Principle

Export bundles must remain reconstructable without reliance on proprietary systems.

---

## Institutional Objective

Prevent governance artefacts from becoming operationally trapped.
